Our Programs

Programs designed to leave institutions stronger.

Four flagship initiatives across faculty development, student capacity-building, social impact, and institutional partnerships — each formalized through an MoU and co-delivered with specialist partners.

Program 01
Faculty Development

MLOps Faculty Development Program

A 3-day, 18-hour hands-on FDP co-delivered with Penlio Edufab Pvt Ltd, walking faculty through the complete MLOps lifecycle: version control, automation, containerization, deployment, and monitoring. Every participant leaves with a fully working end-to-end pipeline.

  • Co-delivered with Penlio Edufab Pvt Ltd
  • Format 3 days × 6 hours, on-campus, lab-based
  • Outcome Joint certification under both seals
  • Audience CS & allied engineering faculty

Student Programs

Innovation & Design Thinking Program

A semester-length program for engineering students that combines structured innovation methodology, design thinking frameworks, and applied project work — bridging classroom learning with the kind of problem-solving employers actually look for.

  • Duration One academic semester
  • Format Workshop modules + applied capstone
  • Outcome Project portfolio + certification

Program 03
Social Impact

AI Hackathon for Government Schools

A district-level hackathon designed jointly with the Belagavi District Commissioner's office and DDPI, introducing government school students to applied AI. Prize money is held as a Fixed Deposit in each winner's name, locked until age 18 and withdrawable only for education.

  • Co-designed with District Commissioner · DDPI Belagavi
  • Audience Government school students
  • Prize structure FDs locked till age 18, education only
  • Goal Convert recognition into long-term opportunity
