What We Do

Three pillars of sustained capacity building.

Every program is co-delivered with industry-grade partners, governed by an MoU, and closed with a jointly-signed outcomes report.

01

Faculty Development

Hands-on, industry-grade FDPs co-delivered with technical specialists — including MLOps, AI/ML, and applied data science — bringing production tooling into the classroom.

View MLOps Program
02

Student Programs

Semester-length Innovation and Design Thinking programs equip engineering students with structured methods and applied project work that translates to employability.

Social Impact
