About the Foundation

A foundation built for sustained, measurable impact.

Karnataka-based. Mission-driven. Programmes delivered through structured partnerships with industry, academic institutions, and district administrations.

Tejomarg Foundation exists to enable sustained, large-scale capacity building in the academic ecosystem — through programmes that leave institutions genuinely stronger than they were before.

The Foundation operates as an external programme provider, bringing structured, industry-grade offerings to engineering colleges, government schools, and partner institutions across Karnataka. Its work spans three streams: faculty development, student programmes, and social-impact initiatives in partnership with district administrations.

Every engagement is governed by a written agreement — typically an MoU — with a defined scope of work, milestone reviews, and a co-signed closure report. The Foundation favours models that convert short-term programmes into long-term opportunity: joint certifications under co-branded seals, technical curricula built with specialist partners, and prize structures that lock recognition into educational outcomes.

Tejomarg works alongside KSST, SDM Institutions, Canara Institutions, MTD, and has formalised its flagship MoU with KLS Gogte Institute of Technology, Belagavi. Programme delivery is co-led with technical specialists — most notably Penlio Edufab Pvt Ltd, Bengaluru — and supported by partnerships with the Belagavi District Commissioner's office and DDPI for social-impact work.

What sets us apart

Five defining characteristics.

01

Mission-driven, not commercial

As a foundation, our orientation is towards long-term institutional impact, not short-term revenue. Every programme is designed to leave the partner institution genuinely stronger than it was before.

02

Industry-grade technical partners

We co-deliver with specialist partners who bring real practitioner experience and production-grade tooling into the classroom — most notably Penlio Edufab Pvt Ltd for MLOps and AI programmes.

03

Structured governance through MoUs

Every engagement is formalised through an MoU with clear scope, defined responsibilities, milestone reviews, and a joint outcomes report. Nothing operates on a handshake.

04

Established institutional credibility

Our track record across KSST, SDM Institutions, Canara Institutions, MTD, and KLS GIT — with partnerships extending into district administrations — gives partners the confidence of engaging a serious, well-regarded organisation.

05

Co-branded outcomes

Certifications, programme materials, and reports are co-branded under the seals of the Foundation, the technical partner, and the host institution — ensuring partner institutions receive appropriate visibility and credit.
